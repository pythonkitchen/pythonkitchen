Python Mailing Lists Constitution


  • Proper english is an absolute requirement [3]
  • Don’t use harsh tones even if you are right [1]
  • Don’t abuse maintainers. They could be on their recovery bed reading your harsh words. [1]
  • Accusing people of asking for upvotes does not result in ban [2]
  • Double standards is allowed for core-devs other than the offender [4]
  • Flamewars not based on technical grounds routinely make people leave or want to leave the list [5]
